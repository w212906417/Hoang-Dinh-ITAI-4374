# 🧠 Hoang-Dinh-ML-Course

## 👤 About Me

Hello, my name is **Hoang Dinh**, and I am a student at Houston Community College pursuing a degree in Artificial Intelligence.

I am passionate about **machine learning, neuroscience-inspired AI, and intelligent systems**. I am especially interested in how biological brain processes—such as memory, attention, and perception—can be used to design more advanced and efficient AI systems.

This repository serves as my **course portfolio**, showcasing my work, experiments, and learning throughout the course *ITAI 4374: Neuroscience as a Model for AI*.

---

## 📚 Course Overview

This course explores the relationship between **neuroscience and artificial intelligence**, focusing on how brain-inspired concepts can improve AI systems.

Topics covered include:
- Neural computation and neuron models  
- Sparse representations and sequence learning  
- Temporal memory and anomaly detection  
- Cognitive systems (memory, attention, emotion, perception)  
- Brain-inspired AI design
